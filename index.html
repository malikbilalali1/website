FREQUENT COURSES


Norani Quaida

The Norani Qaida book is structured in a way that makes it easy for beginners to learn the Arabic alphabet, pronunciation, and basic grammar. It starts with the Arabic alphabet and progresses to simple words and phrases, and then to short Quranic verses. The book also includes exercises and quizzes to test the learner's comprehension and understanding.

Tajweed / Tafseer

Tajweed and Tafseer are two important aspects of learning and understanding the Quran. Tajweed refers to the rules and techniques used for reciting the Quran in the correct and beautiful manner. Tafseer, on the other hand, refers to the interpretation and explanation of the Quranic text.
